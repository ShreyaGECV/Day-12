Culture & Festivals

Banaras is known for its vibrant festivals like Dev Deepawali, Ganga Aarti, and Holi. Music, dance, and art thrive in every corner of the city.Varanasi, a city steeped in history and spirituality, celebrates a vibrant array of festivals throughout the year, many of which are dedicated to the Hindu faith and the sacred Ganges River. Some of the most notable festivals include Mahashivratri, Holi, Dev Deepavali, and the Ganga Mahotsav. These festivals offer a rich tapestry of cultural experiences, from religious processions and rituals to musical performances and vibrant celebrations.

Street Food

Varanasi is renowned for its diverse and flavorful street food scene. Some of the most popular and iconic dishes include Kachori Sabzi, Aloo Kachori, Baati Chokha, Malaiyo, and Tamatar Chaat. Other noteworthy street foods include Choora Matar, Laaiya Channa, and Dahi Chutney Golgappe. For a refreshing treat, Lassi and Thandai are also highly sought after.
